A Plant-Powered Hero!

As you may have seen, traces of climate change are everywhere: striking numbers of heatwaves, droughts, typhoons, and hurricanes. Our ecosystems are threatened, and we are in a planetary crisis.

Source: World Economic Forum

Surging greenhouse gases from energy, agriculture and industrial sectors are the key drivers of climate change. Carbon dioxide catches the world attention as it accounts for three-fourth of the global greenhouse emissions.

Source: Climate Watch

While there are encouraging developments to slash carbon footprint, they are rather time-consuming, complicated and expensive, and thus more or less suitable for climate change long-term solutions.

Source: REN21

There is a simpler, more effective and immediate solution: to tackle methane. This is the second largest greenhouse source that responsible for 40% of global warming since the industrial revolution. It is likewise much more dangerous than carbon dioxide, but lasts considerably shorter in the atmosphere.

Source: UN and EPA

Majority of methane is from agriculture activities, particularly farming of livestock or fishes. Besides, its end-products such as meat, egg and dairy also associate with huge nitrous oxide emission.

Hence, our solution is simple: by switching to plant-based diet, we can immediately save the Earth. There are so many healthy, cost-effective and sustainable plant-based options out there. Everyone can and have the power to be our own hero for this magnificent planet!

Source: VOX

By wisely selecting this great lifestyle, you will not only cut more than 90% of greenhouse gas emission, but also preserve so much water, land and energy! These precious resources can be for other uses, such as planting trees to absorb more greenhouse gases, or building solar/wind farms to replace polluted fossil-fuel plants.

Source: University of Michigan

There are many other awesome benefits of plant-based diet, but I will leave them for your own curiosity and self-exploration.
